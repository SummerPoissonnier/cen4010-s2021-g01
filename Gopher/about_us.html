About us

Hello everyone,

Gopher is about feeling less lonely in this difficult time. We created Gopher so people can have a space to communicate with others with similar interest.

In this website you will find that you can:

  • Communicate via a forum (located in home)
  • View your account
  • Change you account setting
  • You can go to chatrooms based on interests on discord server

Thank you for signing up with Gopher and I hope you enjoy!
